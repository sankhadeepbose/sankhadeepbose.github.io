About

Hello, I am Sankhadeep (শঙ্খদীপ), pronounced /ʃɔŋ.kʰoː.d̪iːp/ . I am a PhD candidate at Vellore Institute of Technology, India, specializing in Thermodynamics, Statistical Mechanics, and Atomistic Simulation. I hold a Master's degree in Nanotechnology and a Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering.

I am part of a collaborative research group working with Dr. Bruno D'Aguanno (University of Lincoln, UK), Dr. Andrea Floris (University of Lincoln, UK), and Dr. Erminia Leonardi (CRS4, Italy). Our research aims to advance the understanding of real nonhomogeneous thermodynamic systems, phase transitions in finite systems, and cross interactions in nanofluids.
